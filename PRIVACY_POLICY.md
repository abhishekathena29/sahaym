# Privacy Policy

**App Name:** Sahaym
**Effective Date:** June 12, 2026
**Last Updated:** June 12, 2026

## Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how the Sahaym mobile application ("the App", "we",
"us", or "our") handles information. We respect your privacy and have designed
the App so that it does not require any of your personal information to function.

**In short: the App does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any
personal data from its users.**

## Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect any personal information or data from you, including
but not limited to:

- Name, email address, phone number, or other contact details
- Age, gender, education, health information, or any other personal details
- Location data (GPS, precise, or approximate)
- Device identifiers, advertising IDs, package or version information, or
  analytics data
- Usage data, behavioral data, or in-app activity
- Photos, contacts, files, or other content from your device
- Payment or financial information

You are not required to create an account or provide any personal information to
use the App.

## No Tracking or Analytics

The App does not use any third-party advertising networks, analytics services,
crash-reporting tools, or tracking technologies that collect personal data.

## External Links

The App may contain links to external websites (for example, our website or
informational pages). These external sites are not operated by us, and this
Privacy Policy does not apply to them. We encourage you to review the privacy
policies of any third-party websites you visit.

## Data Storage

The App does not transmit any information to us or to any external servers under
our control. We do not maintain any database of user information.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone,
including children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your
jurisdiction).

## Security

Because we do not collect or store any personal data, there is no user data held
by us that could be subject to a data breach.

## Your Rights

Because we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information for us
to access, correct, delete, or export on your behalf. You remain in full control
of any information stored locally on your own device.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be
reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. We
encourage you to review this policy periodically.
