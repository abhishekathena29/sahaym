## Full description

Sahaym is a simple, easy-to-use health-awareness app created to support the wellbeing of senior citizens. An initiative by Aaravdeep Sindhu, Sahaym brings trusted health information, advisory videos, and quick access to official public-health resources together in one place — designed with large, clear, senior-friendly screens.

Whether you want to learn healthy daily habits, watch short health-advisory videos, or reach official government health services, Sahaym makes it quick and stress-free.

WHAT YOU CAN DO WITH SAHAYM

• Health Awareness — Stay informed with easy-to-understand health tips and awareness content focused on the needs of senior citizens.

• Health Advisory Videos — Watch curated videos right inside the app, with simple playback controls.

• Photo Gallery & Events — Browse photos and stay updated on health awareness programs and events.

• Quick Links to Official Services — Open the official websites for key public-health services in one tap:
   – ABHA (Ayushman Bharat Health Account)
   – eSanjeevani telemedicine consultation
   – Tele-MANAS mental-health support
   – Ayushman Card beneficiary services (NHA)

• Senior-Friendly Design — Clean layouts, large readable text, and straightforward navigation.

DESIGNED FOR SENIOR CITIZENS

Sahaym focuses on clarity and ease of use, so older adults — and the family members who help them — can find reliable health information and reach the right official services without confusion.

PRIVACY FIRST

Sahaym does not collect, store, or share any personal data. You can use the app without creating an account or entering any personal information. Please review our Privacy Policy for details.

IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER

Sahaym is an independent health-awareness app. It is NOT affiliated with, endorsed by, or authorized by any government entity. Links to official services (such as ABHA, eSanjeevani, Tele-MANAS, and Ayushman Bharat) open the respective official websites operated by their own organizations. The information provided in the app is for general awareness only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for any medical concerns.
